OFFLINE WEB APP

BudgetApp

A private, offline-first household budget PWA with clear expense overviews and spreadsheet import/export.

Highlights

  • Track income and expenses with an at-a-glance budget overview.
  • Import and export Excel files.
  • Install as a PWA and use it offline in the browser.

Privacy

BudgetApp keeps its data in your browser. No account is required and no budget data is sent to a server.
