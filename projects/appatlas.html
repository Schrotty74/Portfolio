NATIVE MACOS APP

AppAtlas

A privacy-focused macOS app for organising personal app collections, metadata and licence information.

Highlights

  • Scan local app folders and bring collections together in one catalogue.
  • Manage metadata, tags, links and licence information.
  • Create local exports with optional encryption.

Privacy

AppAtlas is local first. Personal catalogue and licence data remain on the Mac unless you explicitly export it.
